Nnuewo test

Age Adjusted Mortality rate for age group x to y.

\[aamr= \sum_{i=x}^{y} \Bigg[ \left[ \frac{counts_i}{pop_i}X100,000 \right] X \left[ \frac{stdr_i}{ \sum_{j=x}^{y} stdr_j} \right] \Bigg]\]
